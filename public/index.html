More about Chicago, IL

Chicago is the most populous city in Illinois and the third-most populous in the United States. Known as the "Windy City," it sits on the southwestern shore of Lake Michigan and is renowned for its bold architecture, deep-dish pizza, blues music, and vibrant cultural scene.
